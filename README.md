Exploratory Data Analysis – Tune Sales Data

Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Tune sales data to uncover customer behavior, product performance, and profitability trends to support data-driven business decisions.

Key Insights

Most customers fall into Low and Medium value segments, indicating upselling potential

Adult customers (20–40 years) are the primary sales drivers

Accessories are the top-selling product category by a wide margin

Female and male customers show nearly equal transaction volumes

Lagos and Abuja account for the highest number of transactions

86% of transactions are profitable, indicating strong overall performance

Impact
Insights from this analysis can guide:

Targeted marketing strategies

Inventory and product planning

Regional sales optimization

Tools:
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
